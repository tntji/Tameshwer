Biography

Dr. Tameshwer Nath is an Assistant Professor in the Mechatronics Engineering Department at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur. He completed his Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology, Indore in February 2019, focusing on "Investigations on Contact and Non-Contact Based Actuation Studies of Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) Structures and their Life Cycle Behavior" under the supervision of Dr. I.A. Palani.

Prior to joining IIIT Bhagalpur, he worked as an Assistant Professor at Subharti University, Meerut, and as a Research Associate at CSIR, IIT Indore. He also served as a Teaching Associate at IIT Indore from January 2014 to April 2018.

Dr. Nath earned his M.Tech in Manufacturing Systems Engineering from SLIET, Sangrur in 2012 and his B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Institute of Technology and Management, Gorakhpur in 2008.

His research focuses on Smart Materials, particularly Shape Memory Alloys, Mechatronics, and Additive Manufacturing. He has published numerous research papers in reputed journals and presented his work at various national and international conferences.
